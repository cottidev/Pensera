# 🖊️ Pensera, Your Personal Journal :)

Pensera is a calm, beautiful journaling app that lives right in your browser. No accounts, no cloud, no distractions — just you and your words.

---

## ✨ What it does

- **Write freely** — A clean, minimal editor so nothing gets between you and your thoughts.
- **Stay organized** — Create groups to sort your entries however makes sense to you (by mood, topic, project, whatever).
- **Never lose a word** — Everything saves automatically as you type.
- **Look the way you want** — Switch between light and dark mode, pick your font, and adjust the text size.
- **Focus mode** — Hide the sidebar and write without any distractions.
- **Highlight & color** — Select any text to add highlights or change its color.
- **Keep your data** — Export all your entries as a single file to back them up or move them elsewhere. Import them back just as easily.

---

## 🚀 Getting started

Just open `index.html` in your browser — that's it! No installation, no setup.

If you want to use it like an app on your phone or desktop, you can install it from your browser's menu (look for "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app"). It works offline too.

---

## 📖 How to use it

**Writing an entry**
Click **New Entry** in the sidebar, give it a title, and start writing. Your work saves automatically — you'll see a small "Saved" indicator at the top.

**Organizing with groups**
Click **New** next to Groups to create a group (like "Work", "Travel", or "Ideas"). Then click the **+** button next to any group to move entries into it. Click a group name to see only its entries.

**Searching**
Use the search bar at the top of the sidebar to find any entry by its title or content.

**Changing how things look**
- Use the font and size controls at the bottom of the editor
- Toggle dark mode with the moon/sun button in the top-right
- Hit **Focus** to hide the sidebar and enter distraction-free writing mode

**Keyboard shortcuts**
| Action | Shortcut |
|---|---|
| New entry | `Ctrl/Cmd + E` |
| Duplicate entry | `Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + D` |

**Backing up your journal**
Click **Export** in the Groups panel to download all your entries as a `.json` file. To restore them on another device, click **Import** and select that file.

---

## 🔒 Privacy

Everything stays on your device. Pensera stores your journal in your browser's local storage — nothing is sent to any server, ever.
